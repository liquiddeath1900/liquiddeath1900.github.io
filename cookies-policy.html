Cookies Policy

Last Updated: December 1, 2024

Introduction

This Cookies Policy explains how SeamlessFlow.ai ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website or use our AI-driven business automation services. By using our services, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners. These pieces of information can be used to improve your experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly, including authentication, security, and load balancing.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website, such as Google Analytics cookies.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences, such as language and region settings.
  • AI Service Cookies: Support AI-driven features, such as chatbot session management and workflow states.
  • Marketing and Advertising Cookies: Track your browsing habits to deliver targeted advertising.

Cookie Management

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. Options include:

  • Deleting cookies from your browser
  • Configuring your browser to block cookies
  • Using private browsing mode
  • Opting out of specific tracking cookies

Please note that blocking certain cookies may impact website functionality, personalization features, and AI service performance.

Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party cookies provided by trusted partners, such as analytics services, social media plugins, payment processors, and marketing tools. Each third party provides its own cookie management tools, which can be used to opt out of their cookies.

Updates to Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or service improvements. Significant updates will be communicated through:

  • Website notifications
  • Email updates (for registered users)
  • Service announcements

Cookie Consent

  • Granular cookie preferences selection
