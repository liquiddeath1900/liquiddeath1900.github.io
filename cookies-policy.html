Introduction

This Cookies Policy explains how SeamlessFlow.ai uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our website and with our AI-driven business automation services.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us:

  • Make our website work efficiently
  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve your browsing experience
  • Provide personalized content and services

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality. Cannot be disabled.

  • Authentication
  • Security measures
  • Basic functionality

Performance & Analytics

Help us improve our website through anonymous usage data.

  • Google Analytics
  • Usage patterns
  • Performance metrics

Functionality Cookies

Remember your preferences and settings.

  • Language preferences
  • Region settings
  • Customization options

AI Service Cookies

Support our AI-driven features and automation.

  • Chatbot sessions
  • AI preferences
  • Workflow states

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

Chrome

Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies

Firefox

Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies

Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Cookies

Edge

Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

Your Choices

You have the right to:
