When Should You Use Each?

Start with SEO if:

  • You can wait 4-8 weeks for results
  • You want to build a long-term asset
  • Your budget is under $1,500/month
  • You serve a specific local area
  • You're in it for the long haul

Use Google Ads if:

  • You need leads TODAY
  • You have $2,000+/month to spend
  • You're launching a new business/service
  • You're running a limited promotion
  • High profit margins justify high costs

üí° Pro Tip: Do Both (Eventually)

The smartest strategy: Start with SEO to build your foundation. Once you're ranking well organically, add Google Ads for extra volume. SEO gives you sustainable, cost-effective leads. Ads give you the ability to scale quickly when you need it.
