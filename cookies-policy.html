Introduction

This Cookies Policy explains how SeamlessFlow.ai uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our website and with our AI-driven business automation services. We strive to be transparent about how we use these technologies and provide you with control over your preferences.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They serve several important functions:

  • Make our website work efficiently
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Improve your browsing experience
  • Provide personalized content and services
  • Help us understand how people use our site

Types of Cookies We Use

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Here's how to access cookie settings in popular browsers:

Chrome

Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies

Firefox

Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies

Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Cookies

Edge

Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

Your Choices

You have several options when it comes to managing cookies:

  • Accept or decline non-essential cookies
  • Modify your cookie preferences at any time
  • Delete cookies from your device
  • Use our services with essential cookies only

Please note that blocking some types of cookies may impact your experience of our website.
