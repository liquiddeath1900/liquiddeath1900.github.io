Introduction

This Cookies Policy explains how SeamlessFlow.ai ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website or use our AI-driven business automation services. By using our services, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners. These pieces of information can be used to improve your experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

• Purpose: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly.

  • Authentication cookies
  • Security cookies
  • Load balancing cookies
  • User interface customization cookies

2. Performance and Analytics Cookies

• Purpose: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

  • Google Analytics cookies
  • Usage pattern tracking
  • Error monitoring
  • Performance measurement

3. Functionality Cookies

• Purpose: Remember your preferences and settings.

  • Language preferences
  • Region settings
  • Customized dashboard layouts
  • User interface preferences

4. AI Service Cookies

• Purpose: Support our AI-driven features and automation services.

  • Chatbot session management
  • AI preference learning
  • Automation workflow states
  • Service customization

5. Marketing and Advertising Cookies

• Purpose: Track your browsing habits to deliver targeted advertising.

  • Advertising tracking cookies
  • Social media cookies
  • Marketing campaign effectiveness measurement

Cookie Management

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish:
