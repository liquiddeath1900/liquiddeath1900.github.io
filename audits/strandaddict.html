Critical Issues

These issues are severely impacting your online visibility and must be addressed immediately.

1. NOINDEX Meta Tag Blocking Google Critical

<meta name="ROBOTS" content="NOINDEX">

Impact: Google cannot index your website. You will NOT appear in any search results. Potential customers searching for "braids near me" or "hair salon Lower East Side" will never find you.

Fix: In Squarespace: Settings → SEO → Enable "Allow search engines to index this site". This is likely accidentally disabled.

2. 77% of Images Missing Alt Text High

17 of 22 images have no alt attribute

Impact: Google Image Search can't understand your portfolio. This is a huge missed opportunity - people search for hairstyle inspiration on Google Images constantly.

Fix: Add descriptive alt text to every image. Example: "Goddess locs hairstyle with gold accents by Strand Addict NYC"

3. Duplicate H1 Tags Medium

Found 2 H1 tags: "Strand Addict Studio" and "OUR LOCATION:"

Impact: Confuses search engines about your page's main topic. Should only have one H1 per page.

Fix: Change "OUR LOCATION:" to H2 or H3. Keep "Strand Addict Studio" as the only H1.

4. Sitemap Uses HTTP Instead of HTTPS Medium

Sitemap: http://www.strandaddict.com/sitemap.xml

Impact: Google may have issues following links in sitemap. All URLs should use HTTPS.

Fix: Squarespace should auto-generate HTTPS sitemaps. Check your site URL settings are set to HTTPS.
