Why Cannabis?

No Google/Meta Ads Allowed

Cannabis businesses can't run paid ads on major platforms. That's why organic SEO and AI-powered marketing automation are critical - they're the only scalable ways to reach customers online.

Compliance is Complex

Metrc tracking, seed-to-sale reporting, purchase limits, advertising restrictions - cannabis has more regulatory requirements than almost any other industry. Automation prevents costly mistakes.

Customers Need Education

Cannabis customers have questions about strains, effects, dosing, and products. AI budtenders can answer these questions 24/7, increasing conversions while reducing staff workload.

The Market is Exploding

NYC's cannabis market hit $1.6B in 2025 with 60%+ YoY growth. The dispensaries that adopt AI and automation now will have a significant competitive advantage as the market matures.
