2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners. These pieces of information can be used to improve your experience.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and include authentication, security, and load balancing cookies.

3.2 Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website. Examples include Google Analytics cookies and error monitoring.

3.3 Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow us to remember your preferences, such as language and region settings.

3.4 AI Service Cookies

These cookies support our AI-driven features, such as chatbot session management and service customization.

3.5 Marketing and Advertising Cookies

These cookies track your browsing habits to deliver targeted advertising and measure marketing campaign effectiveness.
