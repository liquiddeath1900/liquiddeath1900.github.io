‚ùå Keyword Stuffing Your Business Name

‚ùå Using a PO Box or Virtual Office

‚ùå Buying Fake Reviews

Google's algorithm detects fake reviews. Penalties include review removal, ranking drops, and suspension. Not worth it.

‚ùå Creating Multiple Listings

One business = one listing. Creating listings for each service area will get them all suspended.

‚ùå Ignoring Negative Reviews

‚ùå "Set and Forget" Mentality

An abandoned profile signals to Google you're not active. Regular updates (posts, photos, responding to reviews) keep you ranking.
