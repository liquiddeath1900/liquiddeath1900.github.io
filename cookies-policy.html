2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to store data that can be recalled by a web server in the domain that placed the cookie. We use cookies and similar technologies for various purposes, including:

  • Enhancing platform functionality
  • Improving AI service delivery
  • Analyzing usage patterns
  • Personalizing user experience
  • Securing our services
