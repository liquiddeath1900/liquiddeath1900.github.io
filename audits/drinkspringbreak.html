Critical Issues

These issues are impacting your search visibility and should be addressed soon.

No Meta Descriptions on Any Page Critical

<meta name="description" content=""> — Missing on all pages

Impact: Google shows random text snippets from your page instead of compelling copy you control. This reduces click-through rates from search results.

Fix: In Wix SEO settings, add unique meta descriptions (150-160 chars) for each page. Example: "Spring Break Vodka Seltzer - Low calorie, real fruit juice seltzers in 4 refreshing flavors. Find your outlet and unplug."

No Schema Markup High

No Product, Organization, or Brand schema detected

Impact: Missing rich search results. No product info, reviews, or brand details appear in Google.

Fix: Add Organization schema and Product schema for each flavor. Wix has built-in schema options in Site Settings > SEO.

Incomplete/Placeholder URLs Medium

/blank-1 and /blank pages exist with unclear purpose

Impact: These look like placeholder pages that may confuse users and waste crawl budget.

Fix: Rename /blank-1 to /store-locator or /find-us. Delete /blank if unused.

Age Gate May Block Crawlers Medium

Homepage shows 21+ age verification before content

Impact: While legally required, some search crawlers may not bypass the age gate.

Fix: Ensure Wix SEO settings allow bots to bypass. Consider letting /vodkaseltzer be your canonical homepage.
